The V- Shaped Student

What is a V-Shaped student?

and who will get the rose?

In our video podcast project, my group and I decided to have a bit of fun with our interpretation of what it means to be a V-shaped student. Our whole video is filmed like it is the lost footage of a season of the show The Bachelor that never aired. In this episode, Tyler and Susie are competing for Geoff’s rose, each of their characters embodying the V-shaped values, or lack there of. Tyler’s character is the embodiment of everything that goes against what a V-shaped student is, while Susie’s character actually embodies all the values that a V-shaped student should have. In the end, the rose can only go to one lucky person, but no spoilers! You will have to watch and find out who gets Geoff’s final rose.
