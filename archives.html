The T- Shaped Student

What does it mean to be T-Shaped

The second project of this semester was the “T-Shaped” podcast project. This project was aimed both at deepening our understanding of what it means to be a “T-shaped student” in the real world. In our podcast our group focused on assessing this concept through a foux radio-talk show, Three College Kids Trapped in a Box, in which my my group members and I share our personal experiences with the T-shaped concept as real Virginia Tech undergraduate students. Through our open dialog one can catch a glimpse of what it is like to be a student studying at Virginia Tech from multiple different perspectives Our main focus in our podcast was to really try an asses if the Virginia Tech administration, as a whole, is succeeding in promoting the T-shaped student or whether there is still work that needs to be done. With all of us coming from different backgrounds and majors, each of us had different throughts and experiences regarding what it means to strive for T-shaped student status as Hokie and by collaborating together and openly discussing this topic, we hope to get the conversation going about what the “T” in a “VT-shaped” student looks like in terms of collegate life.
